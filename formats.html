Aceless Academy Formats

Community-driven alternative Pokémon TCG formats.

Format Philosophy

Aceless formats seek to preserve competitive integrity, interactive gameplay, and creative deckbuilding while removing mechanics that fundamentally alter traditional single-Prize gameplay.

Aceless Standard

Uses the current Pokémon Standard card pool while applying Aceless format restrictions and ban lists.

View Standard Ban List

Aceless Silver

Uses cards with Regulation Mark G and later. This format does not rotate and maintains its own ban list.

View Silver Ban List

Aceless Expanded

Uses cards from the Black & White era onward while applying Aceless restrictions and governance policies.

View Expanded Ban List

Card Release Policy

Aceless Academy recognizes Pokémon's structured release schedule:

  • Prerelease Period
  • Official Product Release
  • Pokémon Play Legality Date

Newly legal cards will generally remain under observation before ban action is considered.

Exceptions may occur immediately if cards create issues involving:

  • Prize Card manipulation
  • Excessive durability or survivability
