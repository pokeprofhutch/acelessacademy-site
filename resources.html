Resource Hub

Aceless Academy maintains this collection of resources to help players learn, improve, and participate in competitive Pokémon TCG.

Resources are organized by source and reliability to help players find trusted information for learning, deckbuilding, and competition.

🔴 Official Resources

Official resources are provided by Pokémon or recognized Organized Play organizations.

  • Pokémon TCG Official Rules and Resources

    Official gameplay rules, updates, and information directly from Pokémon.

  • Pokémon Play! Organized Play Information

    Information about Pokémon events, competitive play, and Organized Play programs.

  • Tournament and Event Information

    Official event information including tournament locations and schedules.

➕ Recommended Resources

These resources are trusted tools used by competitive players for research, deckbuilding, and tournament preparation.

  • Limitless Pokémon TCG

    Tournament results, deck lists, card databases, and competitive analysis.

  • TrainerHill

    Tournament statistics and competitive player data.

  • Slowpo.ke

    Tournament organization tools and resources for Pokémon TCG events.

  • JustInBasil Resources

    Deckbuilding guides, strategy articles, and beginner resources.

  • RK9 Labs

    Tournament software and Organized Play tools.

⭐ Community Trusted Resources

Community resources are player-created references that provide useful information, analysis, and discussion.

  • PokéGym Compendium

    A reference database for Pokémon TCG rulings and card interactions.

  • PokéBeach

    Community news, announcements, and Pokémon TCG discussion.
